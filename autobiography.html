This is My Life

My name is Kieth Bryan Cervantes Mallari, and this is the story of my life—a simple and ordinary journey filled with small moments that shaped who I am today.

Early Life

I was born in the Philippines and raised in a humble, loving family. Life wasn’t perfect, but it was peaceful. My parents taught me values like respect, hard work, and humility. From a young age, I was curious, observant, and content with the little joys in life.

Elementary Days

My elementary life was filled with innocent fun and learning. I studied at a nearby public school and met my first friends. I wasn’t the smartest in class, but I did my best. I joined school programs and enjoyed recess with classmates. Field trips and Christmas parties were especially memorable.
My concerns were simple: homework, projects, and chores at home. Yet, every day felt like an adventure—these years built the foundation of my personality.

High School Life

High school brought challenges and memories. I started to understand myself and the world more. There was academic pressure, friendship excitement, and the confusion of growing up. I wasn’t a star student, but I always aimed to learn and pass.
Joys included school fairs, intramurals, group projects, and bonding with friends. Tough times came with low grades, misunderstandings, and self-doubt. Each experience helped me grow. High school taught me that life isn’t always easy, but every struggle has a purpose.

College Journey

Now, I’m studying at Heroes Memorial College, taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). College is both exciting and intimidating: more responsibilities, harder subjects, and a bigger step toward the real world.
I’ve met new people, faced late-night studying, and challenging projects. I’ve learned about time management, found new friendships, developed new skills, and started to discover my future path.
College life is teaching me independence and responsibility. I know now that success takes patience, effort, and sacrifice.
